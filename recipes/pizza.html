Pizza

Description

This is a classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos. Make perfect pizza at home!

Ingredients

  • dough
  • cheese
  • pepperoni
  • sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 480C
  2. Place sauce on dough
  3. Place pepperoni and cheese on top
  4. Bake for 9 minutes
  5. Serve
